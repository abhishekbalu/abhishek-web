Alert and oxygen flow control system for COPD paitent

PDF Link (block diagram)

The main aim of the project is to make a user friendly and safe system that can help patients when they are on there own. COPD is a fatal disease which should be monitored very closely. For elderly and kids the luxury of time is not provided by adults due to various reasons. This project bridges the gap between the patient and their dear one's. This also help the doctor or the nurse to keep track of their patient in order to give better care.

We use a microprocessor based system which collects real time data from the persons body and calculates various aspects of the body and derives a graph with data against time this data is used to predict the daily routine of the patient or the user. The data collected is also used for two other purposes posting it online for the doctor or the dear one to see and to activate the emergency protocol of regulating the oxygen intake.

The project is now in the development stage where we are prototyping and analyzing the parameters
